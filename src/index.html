Niagara Falls Tourism Project

The Niagara Falls is a place with a long history of rolling barrels and sightseeing attractions. It has been the center of tourism in the area for the past few hundred years. Thousands of people visit the Niagara Falls every year which has helped the once unknown city develop into one bustling with tourism. Many people come to learn about the history of the Niagara Falls and enjoy things like dining in the Skylon Tower or going through the tunnels of an old hydro power station located at the Niagara Falls. There are many more things to do at the Niagara Falls than are listed above. Look through this website to explore the many different experiences you may have at the Niagara Falls.
